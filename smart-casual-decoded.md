# Smart Casual: The Dress Code Nobody Explains

"Dress smart casual." Three words that have caused more wardrobe crises than any other dress code. Too casual and you look underdressed. Too smart and you're the guy who wore a suit to brunch.

Let's fix this.

## The Formula

Smart casual = **one dressy piece + one relaxed piece**. That's it.

- Blazer + Jeans ✅
- Polo + Chinos ✅
- Oxford Shirt + Sneakers ✅
- Kurta + Loafers ✅

## The Must-Haves

### Tops
- **Oxford button-down shirt** — the smart casual MVP
- **Knit polo** — dressier than a t-shirt, cooler than a shirt
- **Light blazer** — unstructured, unlined, breathable

### Bottoms
- **Slim-fit chinos** — in khaki, navy, or olive
- **Dark jeans** — no rips, no fading, minimal branding
- **Tailored shorts** — for summer smart casual (above the knee)

### Shoes
- **White leather sneakers** — clean, minimal
- **Loafers** — instant elevation
- **Chelsea boots** — for when you want to look sharp

## The Indian Context

In India, smart casual gets interesting. A well-fitted kurta with chinos and loafers? That's smart casual done right. Don't think Western-only. Think fusion.
