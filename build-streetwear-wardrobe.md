# How to Build a Streetwear Wardrobe from Scratch

Streetwear isn't about logos and hype. It's about attitude, proportions, and knowing which rules to break. Here's how to build a streetwear wardrobe that actually works — without burning a hole in your pocket.

## Start with the Essentials

Before chasing trends, nail the basics:

- **2-3 oversized tees** in neutral colours (black, white, olive)
- **1 quality hoodie** — heavyweight cotton, not the thin stuff
- **Cargo joggers or relaxed fit jeans** — comfort is non-negotiable
- **A pair of clean sneakers** — white AF1s or their equivalents

## Layer Like You Mean It

The magic of streetwear is in layers:

1. Start with a fitted base tee
2. Add an open flannel or oversized shirt
3. Top with a bomber or denim jacket

Proportions matter. Oversized on top? Keep bottoms tapered. Baggy pants? Keep the top fitted.

## Accessories Make or Break It

- **Caps** — dad caps or beanies depending on the season
- **Chains** — one simple chain goes a long way
- **Bags** — crossbody bags are streetwear essential
- **Socks** — visible socks are a statement, not an accident
